Mobile App designed to reconnect couples to each other.

Our lives are full of responsibilities and stress, we tend to forget about the “little things” that make our relationships happy and healthy.

This app will provide gentle nudges to all types of couples to help them remember thoughtful gestures.

Research

According to a recent survey about the impact that the COVID-19 pandemic has had on marriages, there was a 34 percent increase in divorces in 2020 compared to 2019.

Through conducting interviews and survey testing, we discovered a majority of those tested had felt emotional pain from their partner, from either a current or past relationship. Pain derived from lack of communication, lack of quality time, lack of gift giving and forgetting special dates. These emotions often led to feeling emotionally ignored or forgotten.
